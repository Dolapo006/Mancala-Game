How to Play

Number of players: 2

Layout: Rectangular board with 2 rows of 6 holes, with one long hole on each side of the rows called the “mancala”.

Initial setup: 4 beads are placed in each hole, excluding the mancalas (total of 48 beads). Players have their own rows (player 1 on top, player 2 on bottom), and their own mancala (player 1 on the left, player 2 on the right).

Objective: End with the most beads in your posession

Intstructions:

  • During each player’s turn, they will click on one of their holes to distribute the beads around the board, moving counter-clockwise
  • Marbles will skip over an opponent’s mancala when distributed around the board
  • The game is over when one of the players has no more beads in any of their holes, excluding the mancala
  • The player with the greater number of beads in their posession is the winner. Any holes with beads in them count toward the corresponding player's total at the end of the game.
